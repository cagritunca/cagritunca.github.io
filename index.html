About Me

In March 1989, I saw the mother earth for first time in Ankara. Thanks to my father, i've began to grow deep curiosity against mystery of nature at very first years of my childhood. While society of earth celebrating new millennia, i've mostly created my future career path in my mind. Graduated from Ankara Atatürk Lycee and matriculated to Department of Geological Engineering in Ankara University. Through the years of personal development, i've adopted many field of interests and participated various projects.

I'm a social and talkative person and have a peaceful, compassionate personality. I prefer to resolve problems, not creating them.
